Navigating your Bookshelf

Start by heading to the "Add Books" tab. Search for any book you're reading, want to read, or have already finished, and add it to your collection.

Every time you read, select a book and click "Make an Entry." Write down your thoughts, notes, or a summary of what you've read. Saving an entry automatically updates your reading progress for that book.

Each book is divided into several milestones. By consistently making entries, you'll reach these checkpoints, earning points.
