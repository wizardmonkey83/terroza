How do Challenges Work?

Go to the "Create Challenge" tab and choose a friend and book to challenge.

Once your friend accepts the challenge, the race begins!

As you and your friend read and make entries, your progress percentages will update on the main "Challenges" page.

The first person to complete the book and reach 100% progress is crowned the winner and will recive a bookmark as a sign of their victory. All your past victories and defeats will be recorded in the "Past Challenges" section.
